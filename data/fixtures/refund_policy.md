# Refund Policy

- Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery.
- Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
  after the returned item is received and inspected.
- Shipping charges are non-refundable except when the item arrived damaged or
  the wrong item was sent.
- Any actual refund is issued by a human agent. The triage agent never promises,
  approves, or issues a refund on its own.
